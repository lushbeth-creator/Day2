If I Am Able To, Will You...

You may not walk away with a resolution at the end of the first meeting, and that's okay. Isolate the merchant's concerns by recapping their needs, requirements, and decision making process before you leave.

"During our conversation, I heard you say your biggest concerns were ___. If I am able to resolve those to your satisfaction, will you be ready to move forward with me?"
