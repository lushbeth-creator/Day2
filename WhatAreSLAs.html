What is an SLA?

An SLA, or Service Level Agreement, is Propelr's internal commitment for how quickly a specific type of support ticket gets addressed. Every ticket category, boarding, equipment, underwriting, and more, has its own SLA window attached to it.

Knowing the SLA for a ticket lets you set the right expectation with your merchant up front, instead of guessing at a timeline or promising something faster than what's realistic.

If a ticket is approaching or has passed its SLA, that's your cue to follow up, rather than wait indefinitely and let the merchant wonder what's happening.
