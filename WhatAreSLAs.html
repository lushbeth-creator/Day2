What is an SLA?

An SLA, or Service Level Agreement, is Propelr's internal commitment for how quickly a specific support ticket gets addressed. Every category, boarding, equipment, underwriting, has its own SLA window.

Knowing the SLA lets you set the right expectation with your merchant, instead of guessing at a timeline or promising something faster than what's realistic.

If a ticket is approaching or has passed its SLA, that's your cue to follow up, rather than wait indefinitely.
