Role Play
The Close

Setup: You're back with the merchant a couple weeks after Discovery. You've built a proposal around what you learned, better rates and less time spent on daily reconciliation. They like what they see but say they want to think about it.

Agent goal: Get to a real yes or no today, not a maybe.

How to run it: One person plays the merchant, one plays the agent. Present the solution, address "let me think about it," then ask for the business using the "if I am able to, will you" framework. Swap roles and run it again.
