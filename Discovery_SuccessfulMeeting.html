30 Min · Learn, Don't Sell
Discovery Appointment

Leave with their last 2 to 3 months of processing statements. Your only job on this visit is to understand their world: pain points, process, who decides, and when. You are building a map, not making a pitch.

What Does a Successful Meeting Look Like?
Build Rapport
  • Building rapport is ongoing
  • Starts at the first impression
  • Being curious about the merchant on a personal level helps them open up
Discuss
What's your go-to ice breaker when you walk in somewhere for the first time?
Set the Agenda
  • Set expectations for what you plan to cover
  • Tell them what you want to do and why
  • Be mindful of their time
Discuss
What does a confident, natural agenda sound like in your own words?
Fact-Finding Questions
  • Should be about 70% of your appointment
  • Goal is to uncover what they value and any pains they have
  • Identify what Propelr can genuinely solve
Discuss
What are two or three questions that would actually tell you if there's a real opportunity here?
Qualify Next Steps
  • Find out their timeline for making decisions
  • What are the roadblocks to switching?
  • Which "if I am able to, will you" promises could you use?
Discuss
Think about a real objection you've already heard. What did you do with it, and what would you do differently now?
