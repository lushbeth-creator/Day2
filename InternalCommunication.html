The Teams Behind Your Deal
Underwriting
The underwriting department is responsible for assessing the risk of all merchant applications while ensuring our standards for all applications and risks are followed. Our underwriting team utilizes different processes, procedures, and standards to determine the risk associated with each account, making sound decisions with suitable risk outcomes for Propelr.
Boarding
Our boarding team is responsible for setting up new merchant accounts on Propelr's supported platforms. Our Boarding Specialists ensure all card types, pricing, and equipment are correctly configured so the merchant can process payments in the most efficient manner.
