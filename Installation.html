After the Yes
Installation

The purpose of this section is to help you be prepared with what happens during the installation process. We prepare so much for what happens beforehand and often forget that being knowledgeable about the install can help make everything run smoothly.

Payments
  • A completed e-mail will be sent from the Boarding & Maintenance specialist that has built your file
  • If equipment is being ordered, an e-mail will be sent that includes tracking information
  • Equipment will be delivered to the chosen address
Setting Up the Account
  • Confirm the equipment arrived and matches what was ordered on the deal
  • Schedule the install with your merchant: date, time, and who will be on site
  • Verify the setup matches the deal: pricing, gateway details, and any menu builds or cover sheet items
  • Run a test transaction and close a batch before you leave, and make sure the merchant knows the day-to-day basics
When You Get Stuck
  • The Solutions page on the Employee Portal is your first stop for platform guides and setup resources
  • Open a ticket in Stratus, and remember to attach documents and notes to the ticket, not the lead
  • Call Support for anything urgent or time-sensitive
Visit the Solutions Page Call Support: 888.249.0445
Setting Merchant Expectations
  • Walk them through the timeline from signature to install so nothing feels like a surprise
  • Equipment ships to the address they chose, and they will receive tracking information by e-mail
  • Let them know who will be contacting them and when, so they recognize the outreach
  • Set the follow-up rhythm now: a check-in the day after install, another a week after, and a QAR 4-6 weeks out
