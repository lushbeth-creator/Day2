Discovery Role Play

Pair up with a partner from class. This is a short, focused rep, not the full appointment, just enough to build the muscle for opening a meeting well.

Role Play
The First Visit

Setup: You're meeting the owner of a two location boutique bakery for the first time. They agreed to 30 minutes but seem distracted, a supplier delivery just arrived.

Merchant notes: Busy and a little guarded. They've switched processors before and it was a hassle, so they're not eager to do it again. They're also not entirely sure what they're paying in fees today.

Agent goal: Open the meeting. Build rapport, then set a clear, confident agenda for the next 30 minutes.

How to run it: One person plays the merchant, one plays the agent. Run just the opening, rapport and agenda, then swap roles and run it again.
