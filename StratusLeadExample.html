Stratus Data Entry

When you are in Stratus, go to your lead and check for support notes and tickets.

Getting it right the first time:
  • It's vitally important to correctly enter all fields in Stratus and ensure the data is as accurate as possible to avoid delays
  • Validate the TIN / legal, SSN, business start date, banking details, and ownership percentages for all owners
    • These steps shorten processing time and lead to faster approvals
  • Accurately label all attachments, this is key for our onboarding and API feeds
    • Titles like "IPG image" aren't helpful
  • To resolve possible data issues, navigate to the Online Agreement Forms tab, then the Actions column
  • Add a cover sheet for equipment and gateway details
